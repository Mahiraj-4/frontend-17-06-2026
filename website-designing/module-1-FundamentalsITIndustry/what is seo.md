# What is SEO
SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the practice of improving a website's content, structure, and authority so that it ranks higher on Search Engine Results Pages (SERPs) like Google or Bing. The goal is to attract more unpaid, organic traffic from people actively searching for your information or services.

# How SEO Works
Search engines use automated "crawlers" to scan the internet and evaluate websites based on relevance and trustworthiness. Good SEO helps these programs understand your site better. It focuses on three main pillars:

On-Page SEO:
Creating high-quality content and optimizing page elements like titles, headings, and images around what people are searching for (keywords).

Off-Page SEO:
Building your site's reputation and authority across the web, usually by earning links from other reputable websites.

Technical SEO:
Ensuring your website loads fast, works well on mobile devices, and has a clean structure that search engines can easily navigate.

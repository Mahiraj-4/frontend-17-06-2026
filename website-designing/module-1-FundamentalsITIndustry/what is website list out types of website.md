# What is website
A website is a collection of interlinked web pages housed under a single domain name and accessible via the internet. Websites serve different purposes, ranging from selling products to sharing creative portfolios.

# List out types of website
1. E-Commerce Websites:
Digital storefronts where users can browse and buy products or services directly online (e.g., Amazon or Shopify).

2. Business Websites:
Official online spaces for companies to showcase their services, brand identity, and contact information.

3. Blogs and Content Sites: 
Platforms dedicated to publishing articles, tutorials, or personal stories.

4. Portfolio Websites:
Visual showcases used by creative professionals—such as designers, writers, and photographers—to display their work.

5. Social Media & Community Platforms:
Interactive networks where users connect, share content, and participate in discussions (e.g., Reddit or Facebook).

6. Educational & E-Learning Websites: 
Platforms offering courses, tutorials, or institutional information.

7. News & Media Websites:
Digital sources for breaking news, entertainment, and current events.

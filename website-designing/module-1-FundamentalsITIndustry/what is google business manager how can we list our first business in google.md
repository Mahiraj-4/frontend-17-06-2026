# What is google business manager, how can we list out first business in google
Google Business Manager (now officially called Google Business Profile) is a free tool that allows you to manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps. You can use it to list your address, contact info, and hours, as well as post updates, upload photos, and collect customer reviews.

Listing your first business is a straightforward process that takes just a few steps:
1. Sign In:
Go to the official Google Business Profile page and sign in with your Google Account.

2. Add Your Business:
Type in the name of your business. If it appears in the drop-down menu, select it. If it doesn't, click Add your business to Google.

3. Choose Your Category:
Select the industry or category that best fits what your business does.

4. Set a Location:
Choose whether you have a physical location customers can visit (like a shop) or if you are a service-based business (like a plumber).

5. Add Contact Details:
Enter your business phone number and current website.

6. Verify Your Business:
Google requires you to verify that you are the rightful owner. You will generally be asked to verify via a postcard mailed to your business address, an email, or a phone call.
